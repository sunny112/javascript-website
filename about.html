Flower by honeypot

Flower by honeypot is going to color your world!

Beautifully packaging this gel polish has an amazing range of 63 beautiful colors , Perfect application with great adhesion lasting up to 3 weeks on the natural nail without chipping

Flower by honey pot is highly pigmented gel polish that only requires one coat for great coverage.

Directions of Use

Easy soak off within less than 10mins with no damage to the natural nail. 1. Prep the natural nail 2. Apply soak of base gel cure in lamp for 2mins, UV Led( 30 Seconds). 3.Apply One coat of gel polish color cure for 2mins UV, LED (30Seconds). 4. Apply soak of top gel and wipe of sticky layer for superior shine! cure for 2mins UV, LED (30Seconds)
